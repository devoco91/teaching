Welcome to NCI veterinary services.

We are specialist in veterinary service delivery which include Dog,Cat,goat and all other forms of animals.We also deals in other products and services like sales of animal feed ,drugs and other useful materials needed to keep the animals maintained.

Our products and services are second to none in all veterinary management practices

At NCI Veterinary Clinic our aim is to be the best vet in Dublin. We provide excellent veterinary care for dogs, cats and other small animals. We believe in providing the best medical, surgical, and preventative health care for your pets, all delivered in a warm, welcoming and personable environment. Compassion at all times for you and your pet is a priority. Our surgery opens daily from 8am to 7pm Monday to Friday, and 9.30am to 12:30pm on Saturdays, with patient clinics held each morning, afternoon and evening. To minimise waiting times and improve efficiency we do operate an appointment system and would ask you phone ahead for an appointment time. However, we do strive to see all pets on the day, if required, and of course we will see your pet as a priority if they are urgently ill or have an accident. Outside of our opening hours there is full dedicated emergency cover for your pet, all details of which are on the After-Hours link below
