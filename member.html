Our members

At NCI veterinary services, emergency Responses is in the core of our quality service delivery .We are the provider of personal, health and in-home veterinary products and services. Originally established in Co. Dublin in 1991, we have grown across Ireland over a decades. The team in our monitoring centre is passionate about care and take a sense of satisfaction from knowing they have helped so many of our clients in their time of need. In the last 12 months alone we have taken over 450,000 calls in our monitoring centre and take great pride in having been able to help so many of our clients every day. 99.9% of calls are answered in 30 seconds or less. Our motto is ‘Enjoy Life.

You’re In Safe Hands’ and we really do strive to assist your pet in enjoying your life to the full with the reassurance that we are always here for you, should you need us . Our medical products, technology and services play a key role in helping clients pets and pets with long-term health and care needs to stay healthy.
